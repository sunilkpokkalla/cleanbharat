# CleanBharat Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Overview
CleanBharat is a civic engagement mobile app for India that empowers citizens to report local infrastructure issues (garbage, potholes, drainage) directly to the responsible officials (Ward Officers, MLAs, MPs).

## Core Aesthetic
- **Background:** Clean white
- **Typography:** Modern, Sans-serif
- **Accents:** Professional Blue and Vibrant Green
- **Layout:** High whitespace, premium minimalist, mobile-first.

## Target User Flows

### 1. Citizen Reporting Flow
- **Capture:** User opens the camera to snap a photo of the issue.
- **Auto-Detection:** GPS automatically tags the location and identifies the corresponding administrative ward and officials.
- **Anonymity:** A clear toggle for reporting anonymously.
- **Submission:** Instant upload to the public timeline.

### 2. Civic Accountability Flow
- **Public Timeline:** A "Live" feed of all reports in the area to prevent duplicate reports and show transparency.
- **Resolution:** Officers must upload a "Before & After" photo comparison to close a ticket.
- **Escalation:** If not addressed in 72 hours, the ticket automatically escalates to the next level of authority (e.g., from Ward Officer to Municipal Chairman).
- **Citizen Rating:** Once closed, the reporter rates the quality of the work.

### 3. Performance & Gamification
- **Leaderboard:** Monthly rankings of the "Best Performing" and "Worst Performing" wards based on data.
- **Public Pressure:** Seeing which areas are lagging encourages better performance from officials.

## Initial Screen List
1. **Home Timeline:** Feed of reports.
2. **Report Screen:** Image upload + Category + Anonymous toggle.
3. **Resolution Detail:** Before/After photos + Rating UI.
4. **Monthly Leaderboard:** Ranking of wards.
